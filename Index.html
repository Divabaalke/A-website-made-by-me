About Malaria disease

Malaria is a life-threatening disease spread to humans by some types of mosquitoes. It is mostly found in tropical countries. It is preventable and curable. The infection is caused by a parasite and does not spread from person to person.

Our Organisation

Our Organisation aims for health, especially malaria prevention and treatment, aiming to address the pervasive myths related to malaria and empower communities with accurate information and resources for effective prevention and treatment strategies.

Our Aim

Our aim is to provide basic knowledge to people so that they can easily fight against malaria.Our main work is to aware a large number of people about Malaria disease and educate them about how to find with it.We also burst various myths about Malaria which people assumes right.We also provide differenr method to prevent Malaria disease.
